Our Philosophy

  • Free by default. We rarely build paid features. If a tool is useful, it should be accessible to everyone.
  • Privacy-first. No analytics, no trackers, no data collection. Your input stays in your browser.
  • Open & transparent. Most of our code is public. We believe in learning by sharing.
  • Lightweight & fast. No heavy frameworks. Pure HTML/CSS/JS where possible.
